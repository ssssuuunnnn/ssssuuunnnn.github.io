A few days ago, while returning books at the library, I took a moment to browse the shelves and stumbled upon an interesting book titled "30 Minuten: Umsetzungspower" . Intrigued by its thin volume and the promise of completing it in just 30 minutes, I decided to borrow it and take it home.

The author of the book has thoughtfully highlighted key points (in blue) throughout the content, making it easier for readers who wish to quickly grasp the essence of the book in a shorter time.

The book primarily breaks down the steps to enhance execution power into several chapters: understanding your own working style, identifying corresponding practices based on your style, confirming your attitude, pinpointing your current position, assessing available resources, initiating planning and strategizing, executing diligently, regularly reviewing progress against your goals, making adjustments, and persevering with patience. The author also emphasizes that achieving a sense of accomplishment with each small step forward can be a significant motivator to keep your plans on track. The book employs various tables to help readers list and evaluate their own status.

It took me approximately an hour to finish reading this book. The concepts discussed in the book are somewhat similar to those found in many other self-improvement literature, revolving around setting goals, assessing available resources, planning, taking small steps, practicing patience, and self-evaluation. While the book doesn't delve into specific methods, I found that integrating principles from my knowledge of PMP project management execution techniques could create a more detailed and actionable approach. Achieving your goals is akin to climbing a mountain; it requires a step-by-step approach and cannot be rushed. Although these principles are well-known, the number of people who can effectively execute them is surprisingly limited. Those who are determined and committed to execution often achieve remarkable success.

This book provides a comprehensive overview of its concepts in a short amount of time. However, for a deeper understanding and implementation of these concepts, further study may be required. Nonetheless, it serves as a valuable direction for individuals who want to take action but are unsure of how to start.
